About ToadVille Bay

ToadVille Bay is a community that strives for freedom and peace. ToadVille Bay isn't just a community, but a video game. ToadVille Bay is being developed as a Roblox and Mobile/PC game. GhostVista (me) was inspired by multiple video games, he just saw a problem. All games had pay to win mechanics or had to many DLC, GhostVista wanted a game where you pay once, and everything is there all for free.
